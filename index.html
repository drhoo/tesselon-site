A non-binary spatial logic system for field-based computation.
Filed as a provisional patent on 1 September 2025 at 12:08 AM.

Tesselon proposes a matrix-based logic model using five-state computation, continuous signal paths, and spatial architecture. It invites thinking beyond binary — toward systems shaped more like nature, and less like switches.
